Help? (First Steps)
  • Add your employees in the Employees tab.
  • Optional: You can create new Employee Groups (e.g. Art, Design, Programming) and assign any employee to one or more groups in the Employees tab.
  • Once you are ready, Begin Questionnaire in the Current Questionnaire tab.
  • An email will be sent to each employee with a unique link to take the questionnaire.
  • Whenever you log in (or refresh the page) you can see the updated questionnaire status of each employee in the Current Questionnaire tab.
  • Once all the employees have taken the questionnaire, Close Questionnaire.
  • After you close the questionnaire, you can see the results in the results tab.
